Welcome to SteelEagle

SteelEagle is an intuitive autonomous robot development framework that is designed from the ground up to be accessible and platform agnostic. By using edge computing, SteelEagle robots can leverage enormous computational power without much additional payload, enabling all kinds of novel AI in-the-loop applications. SteelEagle is developed and maintained by the Living Edge Lab at Carnegie Mellon University.
